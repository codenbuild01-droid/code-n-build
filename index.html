About Codenbuild

Codenbuild is a student-led startup formed by engineering students who wanted to make projects simpler, more practical, and less stressful for others. Instead of just selling ready-made work, we focus on helping you actually learn by building.

We provide ready-to-use kits, rental options, and mentor guidance so that you don't just submit a project—you understand it, present it confidently, and feel proud of what you’ve built.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common doubts students have.

Do you give ready-made projects or DIY kits?

We provide DIY kits with guidance, not ready-made submissions. You will build the project yourself with our help, which keeps you safe with college rules and helps you actually understand your work.

I don’t know much coding. Can I still do a project?

Yes. We recommend beginner-friendly kits and guide you step by step. You’ll get code templates, explanations, and doubt support so you can learn while building.

How does rental of components work?

You rent components like Arduino, sensors, and driver modules for a fixed period. A refundable security deposit is taken, which is returned when you return all parts safely.

Will you help with PPT, report and viva questions?

Yes. Along with the kit, we support you with explanation, basic documentation structure and viva preparation so you can present confidently.
